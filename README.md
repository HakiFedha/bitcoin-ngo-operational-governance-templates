# Bitcoin Operational & Governance Templates for NGOs

A collection of open-source policy templates, governance frameworks, and operational guides designed specifically for non-governmental organisations (NGOs) and civil society actors seeking to integrate Bitcoin into their treasury management safely and securely.

---

> ⚠️ **Project Status: Work in Progress**
> HakiFedha is an evolving project. These templates serve as foundational baselines and should be adapted to match your organisation's specific legal, regional, and operational requirements.

---

## Repository Directory

To make these resources actionable, the templates are split into core operational areas:

*   📂 **[Bitcoin Governance Framework](./governance/bitcoin_governance_framework.md)** — Defines internal organisational controls, spending limits, authorisation levels, and board-level oversight policies.
*   📂 **[Treasury Management for NGOs](./treasury/treasury_management_guide.md)** — Strategies for managing volatility, balancing local currency needs, and determining operational reserves.
*   📂 **[Custody & Key Management](./custody/custody_key_management.md)** — Technical guidelines for setting up multi-signature wallets, securing backup phrases, and institutional access control.
*   📂 **[Compliance & Audit Checklist](./compliance/compliance_audit_checklist.md)** — A practical tracking tool for accounting teams to ensure transparent record-keeping and local regulatory alignment.
