# Template: Bitcoin Accounting & Recordkeeping Log

> Some Bitcoin wallets such as Sparrow, Specter Desktop, and Electrum can automatically export transaction histories in CSV format, making it easier to track payments, dates, receipts, and wallet activity without starting from scratch. These tools can help organisations maintain accurate on-chain records that can later be used for reporting and audit purposes.
  However, even with these tools, organisations still need a structured recordkeeping format to add context such as transaction purpose, donor restrictions, internal approvals, and budget line classification. This is why the template below remains essential as a complementary organisational tool for proper financial governance.

## How to use this template
Copy this log into a spreadsheet. Complete one row for every Bitcoin transaction your organisation makes or receives. Keep this record updated in real time rather than reconstructing it later. This log is your primary evidence for donor reporting, audit, and tax purposes.
